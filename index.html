Jasce is an electronic music producer based in Philadelphia, PA. She writes indie electronic, indie dance music, midtempo and indie pop. Her sound is influenced by her time studying violin in conservatory, her love for singing and life long passion for electronic dance music. Her sound brings rhythm and melody together to create a familiar yet unique experience
